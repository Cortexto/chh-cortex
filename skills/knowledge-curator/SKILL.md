# CHH Knowledge Curator

Use when moving durable knowledge from `raw/` into `wiki/`.

## Required Reads

1. `CORTEX.md`
2. `wiki/partnerships/chh.md`

## Workflow

1. Confirm curator approval for promotion
2. Check for duplicate wiki pages before creating new files
3. Every promoted fact needs `Source(s)` pointing to `raw/` path
4. No PII/PHI in shared wiki

## Guardrails

- Do not create person-observation or negotiation-mining pages
- Do not edit `TASKS.md` without human approval
