# Demo LSRI workbench — Donor dossier

**Status:** `demo` — for learning donor lens workflow  
**Gate:** Use before drafting external-facing sections

## Must-prove list

1. **Bold, high-risk/high-reward** — not incremental program expansion
2. **AI / data science relevance** where applicable
3. **Life sciences / public health** bridge
4. **Credible follow-on funding** path
5. **JHU faculty team** capability and environment

## Kill list

1. External org as **primary funded implementer** at consortium scale
2. Trainee listed as **co-PI** when rules forbid
3. Conflation with a **separate large team bid**
4. Unverified conversation or corpus statistics
5. Clinical deployment claims the budget cannot support

## Reviewer check

Internal committee — academic rigor, novelty, trainee benefit, multidisciplinary team.

## If I were the reviewer

They fund **new evidence modalities** and **AI-relevant health science**, not generic chatbot pilots. They punish incremental “more of the same ops” unless tied to a clear scientific gap.
