## Reviewer tone

Bold, high-risk/high-reward over incremental; **AI + data science relevance**; translational population-health impact; credible follow-on funding path.

## Boundaries (kill at draft time)

- Do not position external NGO as **primary funded implementer**
- Do not conflate with a separate **large team expression of interest**
- No unverified dataset statistics
- No PII/PHI without IRB frame
