# CHH grant voice

## Register

- Faculty-facing: precise, evidence-first, JHU environment credible
- Center admin: operational, deadline-aware, no hype
- Donor-facing: answer the question first; mechanism before eloquence

## Avoid

- Generic chatbot language
- Unverified population statistics
- Clinical deployment without IRB frame
- Literary vignette openings on institutional forms

## Honesty

Mark uncertain claims `verify` or `needs source`. Curator approves before paste-ready text.
