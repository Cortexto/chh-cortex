# CHH grant writing process

## Stages

1. **Intake** — land RFP, criteria, and notes in `raw/proposals/<slug>/`
2. **Route** — fill `DECISION-CHART.md` (locks, gates, blockers)
3. **Donor lens** — maintain `DONOR-DOSSIER.md` (must-prove, kill list)
4. **Draft** — one section at a time via `skills/grant-writer/SKILL.md`
5. **Red-team** — `skills/grant-red-team-reviewer/SKILL.md` before paste
6. **Revision** — human feedback → `REVISION-LOG.md`

## Pilot default

Internal RFP packaging with curator-owner. See `wiki/partnerships/chh.md`.
